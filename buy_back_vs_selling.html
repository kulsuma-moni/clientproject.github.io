Currently, Disney is only making offers to buy back memberships at 6 resorts. In our experience we have learned Disney will offer members about 35% of theirretail price.

Most sellers will receive an additional $4,000 to $10,000 selling with DVC Sales. If you still think selling back to Disney is a good idea, contact DVC Sales for our "Buy Today" price. In most cases we will make you an offer to purchase your membership and netting you approximately 20% more than Disney buying your membership back.

Our resale value calculator is a great tool to help guide you to the appropriate resale value, but the listing price is entirely up to you!

There are several factors we take into consideration when helping sellers determine the value of their Disney contract. The information above is key to helping us determine the appropriate price in the current market.

It’s also helpful to take a look at our listing page. There you’ll find other contracts that may be similar to yours and you will see their price point. Keep in mind that buyers like to negotiate- so we recommend leaving yourself a few dollars as a ‘buffer’ which will allow you to move down in price.

Do you have points available for the current year? The buyer will reimburse you at closing for the number of points currently available. They get to use the points, so they pay the dues.

We have over 25 years of experience selling directly for the Disney Vacation Club. With over 1,000 visitors to our site every day, most contracts sell within 3-4 weeks (depending on resort, point availability and of course, price) and close 45 days or so later. Our title company handles all money that changes hands, and we get paid when you do… at closing.

Lastly, if you’d like to learn more about DVC Sales and how we do business, I encourage you to select the ‘testimonial’ link below. If you are ready to list your contract for sale, simply click on the ‘list my membership’ button.

For any additional questions, please call or email us. We look forward to working with you!
