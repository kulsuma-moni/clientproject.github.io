Royal Treatment Awaits at Disney’s Grand Floridian Villas

February 12, 2020

With luxurious Disney Vacation Club accommodations, five-star dining and plenty of amenities, staying at Disney’s Grand Floridian Resort and Spa is the perfect way to treat your family like royalty while visiting Walt Disney World. Only a short monorail ride to the Magic Kingdom, the Grand Floridian is styled with old-world charm, a reminder of the days when Florida was first becoming a vacation destination for the well-to-do.

DVC Villas at the Grand Floridian include the modern amenities you’d expect from a DVC resort with well-appointed finishes. However, the rooms are only the beginning of what makes the Grand Floridian a great place to stay or call home!

DINING

The Grand Floridian is known for its truly exceptional dining experiences. From world-class, five-star restaurants to character dining, the Grand Floridian has it all!

VICTORIA & ALBERT’S

The most exclusive and finest dining on Walt Disney World property is located at Victoria & Albert’s. It has won various awards, including the AAA Five-Diamond award and the five-star rating from Forbes’ Travel Guide. This dining experience is best for celebrating those special moments, from milestone anniversaries to marriage proposals.

CITRICOS

Another fine-dining option, Citricos blends American and Mediterranean flavors. The restaurant offers an award-winning wine list as well.

NARCOOSEE’S

Narcoosee’s offers dining inside a large gazebo overlooking Seven Seas Lagoon. End your romantic evening by watching the fireworks from Magic Kingdom (the music may play out on the veranda) and the Electrical Water Pageant!

1900 PARK FARE

1900 Park Fare has buffet character dining for both breakfast and dinner. At breakfast, beloved characters like Winnie the Pooh, the Mad Hatter, Alice, and Mary Poppins may greet you, while dinner is a royal occasion with guests like Cinderella, Prince Charming, and the Tremaine family.

Experiences at the Grand Floridian

At The Grand Floridian, everything about your stay is a luxurious experience. Upon entering the main lobby, you may be greeted with the sounds of a pianist or jazz band. Take a ride on the glass elevator or shop for designer clothing and accessories at the many stores within the main building. You’ll also want to visit these special places at the resort:

SENSES SPA

Getting pampered at this full-service spa at the Grand Floridian is a great way to relax on a non-park day. Indulge in facials, massages, manicures, and haircuts. Or, book a Character Couture experience for a character-inspired makeover, open to guests of all ages!

BIBBIDI BOBBIDI BOUTIQUE

Children ages 3-12 can experience being magically transformed into princesses and knights at the Bibbidi Bobbidi Boutique’s new location inside the Grand Floridian Resort and Spa. Give your little princess a royal evening by visiting the Boutique and enjoying a fairytale dinner at 1900 Park Fare.

TEA PARTIES

The Grand Floridian’s elegant setting makes it the perfect place for a spot of tea. The resort is host to three separate tea party experiences, each with a unique theme. Adults can sample various teas and enjoy a variety of treats, while princesses at heart can sip and snack with Princess Aurora, or children ages 4-12 can attend an un-birthday tea party.

Planning your Disney Vacation Club stay at the Grand Floridian

Calling the Grand Floridian home is great if you love to travel in luxury whenever you visit. It also has the lowest DVC dues at Walt Disney World. You can learn more about planning your DVC Grand Floridian vacation on our website.

Bank your points and treat your entire family to a two-bedroom or Grand Villa during early December (Adventure season). Visiting at that time of year allows you to experience all the magic of the holidays at WDW with fewer crowds. Take in the smells of fresh gingerbread from the life-size gingerbread house and pose for family photos by the stunning Christmas tree. Then, hop on the monorail for Mickey’s Very Merry Christmas Party!
